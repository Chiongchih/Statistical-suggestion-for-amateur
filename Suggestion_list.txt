SS1 Student's t test
    Student's t test need your data to follow a normal distribution, if not or you're not sure about it, you should choose Wilcox's rank sum test instead.
----=-=----
SS2 Correlation analysis
    Pearson's rank correlation analysis need your data to follow a normal distribution, if not or you're not sure about it, you should choose Spearman's rank correlation analysis instead.
